PricePeek

Track product prices by category, save product links and timestamps, find cheapest options and export CSV on demand — lightweight Rust CLI.

Interactive menu 
1) Add product price
2) List all prices
3) Show cheapest option
4) Export data to CSV
5) Delete a product
6) Exit

Why PricePeek?

Designed for simplicity — ideal for quick price comparisons, demos, teaching, or personal shopping lists. Minimal UI, maximum portability.

Add & Categorize

Store product, category, price and link with timestamp.

List & Filter

View all entries or filter by category.

Cheapest

Identify lowest price overall or within a category.

Export CSV

Explicit confirmation before export; choose category or all.

Quickstart

cargo build --release
Download the pre-built Windows binary from the Releases page and run it directly.
Repository: github.com/USERNAME/REPO
Download (Windows .exe): pricepeek.exe
Replace USERNAME and REPO with your GitHub account and repository name to activate links.
